03Aedis - hotel software

Aedis

At Codeva, we embarked on our most ambitious project to date: Aedis, a comprehensive hotel management system. This cutting-edge solution encompasses a full range of functionalities to enhance both guest experience and hotel operations.
Aedis features a robust reservations page, allowing guests to book their stays effortlessly. Complementing this, we developed a mobile app for guests, built with Flutter, enabling them to make reservations, manage their bookings, and utilize an innovative NFC key to open their room doors—an entire model of which we designed and implemented.
The system also includes an admin panel, also developed in Flutter, providing hotel staff with powerful tools to manage reservations, oversee hotel operations, and streamline workflows. Additionally, we created a dedicated app for hotel workers, facilitating seamless communication and task management.
Aedis represents a comprehensive, state-of-the-art solution that significantly elevates the hospitality experience for both guests and staff, showcasing Codeva’s commitment to innovation and excellence in software development.
